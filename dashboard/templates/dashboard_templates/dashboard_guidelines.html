VOTING GUIDELINES

Welcome to Elected!

We are committed to providing a secure, transparent and user friendly voting platform. Please take a moment to read through the guidelines below to ensure a smooth voting experience.

1. Prepare to Vote

・Ensure that you are eligible to participate in the current election.

・Have your credentials ready, such as your username and passowrd or voter ID.

2. Accessing the Ballot

・Log in to your Elected account.

・Navigate to the "Current Elections" section to find the active ballot.

・Review the list of candidates or options available for voting.

3. Casting Your Vote

・Select your preferred candidates or options by clicking on them.

・Check your choices to make sure they're correct.

・Once you're confident, click "Submit" to finalize your vote.

4. Confirming Your Vote

・After submission, you will see a confirmation message indicating that your vote was successfully recorded.

・Remember, once your vote is submitted, it cannot be changed.

5. Maintaining Anonymity and Security

・Your vote is anonymous-no one, not even administrators, can see who you voted for.

・Do not share your account information with anyone. If you suspect any suspicious activity, contact support immediately.

6. Respectful Voting Conduct

・Vote independently and avoid outside influences.

・Respect others' choices and do not attempt to view or interfere with others' votes.

7. Getting Assistance

・If you encounter issues accessing the ballot or submitting your vote, reach out to support or refer to the FAQs for help.
